Yiren Jian

I'm a 5th PhD student in Computer Science working under the supervision of Soroush Vosoughi at Dartmouth College on Machine Learning. During my PhD study, I have been research scientist interns at NEC Labs America , Snap Research and ByteDance AML.

In my first 3 years at Dartmouth, I worked with Lorenzo Torresani on Computer Vision. Prior to attending Dartmouth, I earned a MS in Physics specialized in Computational Biophysics with Chen Zeng and Yunjie Zhao at The George Washington University.

Publications

My current research is focused on Multi-Modal Learning and Low-Shot Learning, i.e., learning visual and language models, and learning from limited labeled data. Meanwhile, I also work on applications of ML in Computational Biology.
